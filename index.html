Biography

I was born and raised in Moscow, Russia. From childhood he was fond of design and drawing and at 18 he went to work in a small design studio. Gradually grew from a simple designer to an art director in major advertising agencies in Moscow.

Art director in field of web and graphic design with more than 14 years of experience. I’m very passionate for creation of outstanding web and graphic design solutions. I incorporate strong analytical, visual and leadership principles into each project I’m working on: from simple and elegant logo design to sophisticated cloud based B2C product.

Confident user of almost all Adobe programs: Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom, InDesign, Flash. I am able to draw "by hand", a lot of experience in academic drawing with pencil, markers, as well as follow the experience of drawing in watercolor and oil. Can use a graphics tablet for a computer like Wacom.

Three years ago I moved to the United States and began to "pump" my programming skills. Currently graduated from college and have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Java. I am on the way in bachelor's degree in Green River College in Web Development. Can do frameworks with Bootstrap. Have experience as Front End Developer. I did few projects in UX/UI.
